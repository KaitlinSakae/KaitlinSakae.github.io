Introduction

Plant Town SF

Plant Town is a local plantshop in San Francisco, CA. The business was first started in 2018 and is located in Hayes Valley. The shop is bright, funky, and is great for both locals and visitors from out of town.

Target audience

Plant Town's target audience is younger people who love plants. Typically Gen-Z and millenials who care strongly about aesthetics and social media content. The whimsical design of the storefront and website are made to invoke feelings of joy and positivity in the customers.

Plant Town is an all inclusive business that aims to make plant parenting an affordable and community building activity for all people! In an effort to give back, Plant Town donates a portion of their profits to local non-profit organizations in the San Francisco Bay Area.

About

Plant Town was first established in 2018 by Kaitlin Sakae, a design and plant obsessed human. Sakae's dream was to build a plant community that was affordable to all and all inclusive. Within the past few years, plant ownership has become a niche practice, so Sakae hopes to make the plant industry back to its roots and make plants earthy again!
