Introduction

The Wall

“The Wall” is a digital publication that encompasses all things related to indoor and outdoor climbing. Climbing has increased in popularity in the last few years and is a leader in the outdoor industry. The sport is accessible to all ages and has room for fast growth both mentally and physically. This website can help climbers of all levels gain a better understanding of the sport, gear expertise, and climbing destinations to visit.
